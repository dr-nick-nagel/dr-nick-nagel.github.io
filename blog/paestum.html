Our Encounter with the Gorgon!

Even though I do a lot of technical blogging I've also always liked to blog a bit about my travels. This past summer I had the good fortune to visit my long time friend in Italy. While there we visited Paestum -- a major ancient Greek city on the coast of the Tyrrhenian Sea. It was there where we encountered the fearsome Gorgon!...

Paestum

Paestum was a major ancient Greek city on the coast of the Tyrrhenian Sea, in Magna Graecia.

The ruins of Paestum are famous for their three ancient Greek temples in the Doric order dating from about 550 to 450 BC that are in an excellent state of preservation. The temples were in honor of Athena, Poseidon and Hera. We encountered the Gorgon at the temple of Poseidon.
