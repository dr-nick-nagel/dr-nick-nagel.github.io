Hello World!

Self portrait in SVG (2024)

My name is Nick Nagel and this is my website. So, who am I? A generalist with decades of experience in the field of information processing I've worn many hats over the years. But mostly I would call myself an information scientist. I earned my PhD in cognitive science from Florida Atlantic University back in the mid nineties and have been steeped in the research, design and development of information systems for the Internet and World Wide Web ever since. Passionate about art, science and lifelong learning, I have a wide range of interests and activities. Consequently, here you can expect an unabashedly heterogenous collection of posts stemming from a range of topics. But that's what I've always loved about the way the WWW works. You never know what will bubble up and potentially help someone, somewhere, somewhen ...
